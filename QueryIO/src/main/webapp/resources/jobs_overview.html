Jobs

The Jobs section lets you manage, schedule and view status/history of your MR jobs.

Job Manager

Here you can define your tagging or custom MR jobs. Also add new jobs, or delete and modify existing jobs

Begin here by clicking on Add button in the Job Manager.

History

The history view lists the MR jobs run in the past in a tabular format, with links to see MR container logs for debugging.

Go to History View.

Status

Status section shows the running/completed status of your scheduled tasks (Queries/Tagging jobs/Custom jobs).

Click here to check the status.
